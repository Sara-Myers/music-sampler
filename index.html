Enhypen Music Sampler

Enhypen is a seven-member K-Pop boy group under Belift Lab. Their discography includes a variety of genres such as R&B, Pop, EDM, and even Chicago Drill. What I particularly love about Enhypen's music is their ability to tune into trendy, pop-influenced melodies but also make each of their songs sound unique to the group through their powerful vocals and distinct tones.
